## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Honeywell All Door Locks

Choosing the right door lock is a critical decision for your home's security. Before you make a purchase, consider these key factors to ensure you select a Honeywell lock that perfectly matches your needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, determine where and how the lock will be used. Is it for a high-traffic front door, a less-used back door, or an interior bedroom or office? For a front door, a keyless entry system with multiple user codes offers maximum convenience. For an interior door, a simple, stylish keyed lever might be sufficient. Always measure your door's thickness, backset (distance from the door's edge to the center of the lock hole), and cross-bore diameter to ensure the new lock will fit without modification.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Honeywell offers a wide range of locks at various price points. A standard mechanical knob or lever can cost between $30 and $60. For an electronic keypad deadbolt or lever, expect to spend between $80 and $150. Set a realistic budget based on the features you need.
