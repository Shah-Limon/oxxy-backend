## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Cordless Handheld Vacuum Pet Hair

Choosing the right cordless handheld vacuum involves more than just picking the one with the highest power. Consider these factors to find a model that perfectly suits your needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Think about where you'll use the vacuum most. Do you need it primarily for furniture and stairs? Or is cleaning your car the top priority? If you have mostly carpeted surfaces, a model with a motorized brush attachment is essential. For tight spaces and cars, look for one with a good crevice tool and maybe a flexible hose.
