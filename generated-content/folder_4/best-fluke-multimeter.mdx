### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, identify your primary application. Are you a commercial electrician who needs non-contact voltage detection and ghost voltage elimination (like the Fluke 117)? Are you an industrial technician working with variable frequency drives who needs a low-pass filter (like the Fluke 87V)? Or are you an automotive specialist who requires millisecond pulse width measurements (like the Fluke 88V)? A hobbyist or DIYer might only need basic voltage and continuity checks (like the Fluke 101). Aligning the meter's feature set with your daily tasks is the most crucial step.
