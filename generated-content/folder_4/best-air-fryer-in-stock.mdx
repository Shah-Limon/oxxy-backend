## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Air Fryer In Stock

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you buy, consider what you'll be cooking and for how many people. Are you a single person or a couple looking for a quick way to crisp up leftovers and frozen snacks? A compact 4-5 quart model might be perfect. Are you a family of four or more? Look for a 6-10 quart model or a dual-basket design to cook a main and a side simultaneously. If you want to replace multiple appliances, a large countertop oven-style air fryer with functions like bake, roast, and rotisserie offers the most versatility.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
