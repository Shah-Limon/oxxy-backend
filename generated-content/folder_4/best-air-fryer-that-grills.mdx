## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Air Fryer That Grills

Choosing the right air fryer grill combo involves more than just picking the one with the most features. Consider these crucial factors to ensure you select a model that perfectly fits your lifestyle and cooking needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, think about how you plan to use the appliance. Are you primarily looking for a powerful indoor grill that can also air fry, like the Ninja Foodi series? Or do you need a versatile countertop oven that can toast, bake, and grill, such as the Cuisinart or Emeril models? If you cook for a large family, capacity will be a top priority. For individuals or couples, a more compact unit might be a better fit. Consider the types of food you cook most often to guide your decision.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($100-$300)
Air fryer grills come in a wide price range, typically from around $100 to over $300. Basic models like the Ninja AG301 offer excellent core performance at a lower price. Mid-range options, often around $150-$250, might include larger capacities or additional features like a smart thermometer. High-end models can offer massive capacity, advanced customization, and premium materials. Set a realistic budget based on the features that are most important to you.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look for key features that enhance performance and convenience. A high maximum temperature (500°F is ideal for searing) is crucial for authentic grilling. Multiple cooking functions add versatility. Features like a smart thermometer (Ninja Smart XL), a removable basket divider (Ninja FlexBasket), or adjustable heating elements (Nuwave Bravo) can significantly improve your cooking experience. Also, check for an effective smoke control system if you live in an apartment or have a sensitive smoke detector.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
An appliance you use regularly should be built to last. Look for models with a sturdy construction, preferably with stainless steel components. The quality of the nonstick coating is also critical for both cooking performance and longevity. A ceramic coating is often more durable and a healthier option than traditional nonstick surfaces. Check user reviews for feedback on the long-term durability of the appliance and its accessories.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
Sticking with reputable brands like Ninja, Cuisinart, and Cosori is often a safe bet. These companies have a proven track record of producing reliable kitchen appliances and generally offer better customer support and warranty coverage. Established brands invest more in research and development, leading to innovative features and better overall performance.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
Finally, consider the appliance's footprint. These are not small gadgets, and they will take up significant counter space. Measure your available space before buying, and also think about where you will store it if you don't plan to leave it out. Models like the Emeril Lagasse 26-quart oven are very large, while more compact grill-style units like the Ninja Foodi have a smaller, though taller, profile.

## FAQ

### What is the best air fryer that grills for beginners?
For beginners, the Ninja Foodi 5-in-1 (AG301) is an excellent choice. It offers fantastic grilling and air frying performance with simple, intuitive controls. It's not overloaded with complex features, making it easy to learn and master the basics of indoor grilling and air crisping. Its proven reliability and more accessible price point make it a great entry into the world of multi-functional cookers.

### How much should I spend on air fryer that grills?
You can expect to spend between $120 and $300. Budget-friendly models with core functions typically fall in the $120-$180 range. Mid-range models with more features, larger capacity, or smart technology usually cost between $180 and $250. Premium, high-capacity ovens with extensive customization options can exceed $250. Your ideal budget depends on the features and capacity you need.

### What features are most important in air fryer that grills?
The most important features are a high maximum temperature (at least 450°F, ideally 500°F for good searing), an effective smoke control system, and quality nonstick surfaces for easy cleaning. Versatility is also key, so look for multiple cooking functions like roast, bake, and dehydrate. For added convenience, features like a smart thermometer or dual-zone cooking can be game-changers.

### How do I maintain my air fryer that grills?
Maintenance is crucial for longevity. Always clean the grill grate, crisper basket, and cooking pot after each use. Most models have dishwasher-safe parts, but handwashing can extend the life of the nonstick coating. Regularly wipe down the interior and exterior of the unit. Make sure to clean the splatter shield, as grease can build up there and cause smoke.

### Are expensive air fryer that grills worth it?
Expensive models are often worth it if you will use their advanced features. A smart thermometer, for example, is invaluable for someone who frequently cooks expensive cuts of meat. Larger capacity and greater versatility can justify the cost for big families or avid home cooks. However, if you only need basic grilling and air frying, a more affordable, high-quality model will serve you just as well.

### What brands make the best air fryer that grills?
Ninja is a clear leader in the dedicated air fryer grill category, known for its powerful Cyclonic Grilling Technology. Cuisinart is a top contender in the toaster oven combo space, offering excellent versatility and performance. Other reputable brands include Emeril Lagasse for high-capacity models, Nuwave for advanced customization, and Cosori for fast and quiet air fryers.

### How long do air fryer that grills typically last?
With proper care and maintenance, a good quality air fryer that grills should last for 3 to 5 years of regular use. The lifespan can be affected by the frequency of use and how well it's cleaned. The heating element and fan are the most critical components. Choosing a model from a reputable brand with a good warranty can provide peace of mind.

### Can I find good air fryer that grills on a budget?
Yes, you can absolutely find great options on a budget. The Ninja Foodi 5-in-1 (AG301) is a prime example. It's an older model but still offers the core high-performance grilling and air frying technology that made the brand famous. By forgoing the newest bells and whistles, you can get a powerful and reliable appliance at a very reasonable price.

### What's the difference between an air fryer grill and a regular air fryer?
The main difference is the ability to achieve high-heat searing. An air fryer grill is equipped with a specific grill grate and technology (like Ninja's Cyclonic Grilling) to reach temperatures of 500°F, creating authentic char marks and flavor. A regular air fryer typically maxes out around 400°F-450°F and is designed for crisping and browning, not true grilling.

### Can you use metal utensils in an air fryer grill?
It is strongly recommended to avoid using metal utensils. The grill grates, baskets, and pots almost always have a nonstick coating. Using metal utensils can scratch and damage this coating, which will ruin its nonstick properties and could cause it to flake off into your food. Always use silicone, wood, or heat-resistant plastic utensils to protect the surfaces.
