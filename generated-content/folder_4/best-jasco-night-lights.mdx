## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Jasco Night Lights

Choosing the right night light involves more than just picking one off the shelf. To find the perfect fit for your home, consider these key factors to ensure you get the safety, convenience, and ambiance you're looking for.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, think about where and why you need a night light. A bedroom or nursery benefits from a soft, warm, or amber light that won't disturb sleep, like the GE SleepLite models.
