## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Von Duprin Doors

Choosing the right Von Duprin parts is crucial for ensuring the safety, security, and functionality of your doors. Here's a guide to help you select the best components for your needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before anything else, identify the exact model or series of your Von Duprin exit device (e.g., 22, 33/35, 98/99). Parts are not interchangeable, so compatibility is non-negotiable. Consider the application: is the door in a high-traffic school hallway, a secure data center, or a standard office? A high-abuse environment will require impact-resistant components, while a secure area may need a fail-secure electric strike.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
Von Duprin parts are premium products, and their prices reflect their quality and reliability. Small replacement parts like end caps or dogging keys can range from $10-$30. More complex mechanical kits, like a top latch assembly, might fall in the $50-$150 range. High-tech components like heavy-duty electric strikes or power transfer units can cost anywhere from $200 to over $500. Set a budget, but prioritize quality for life-safety hardware.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look for features that match your needs. For electric strikes, check the voltage (e.g., 24VDC), operation (fail-safe vs. fail-secure), and strength ratings (static and dynamic). For mechanical parts, confirm the material (e.g., steel, durable polymer). For replacement kits, ensure they are complete and include all necessary screws and pins. Always look for parts that are fire-rated if they are being installed on a fire door.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Always opt for genuine OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) Von Duprin parts. These are made from the same high-quality materials and to the same specifications as the original hardware. Look for solid metal construction over plastic where possible, especially for load-bearing or impact-prone components. For electronic parts, check for endurance ratings, often listed in cycles (e.g., 1,000,000 cycles), which indicate a long operational life.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
Von Duprin, part of Allegion, is a world leader in exit devices and access control. Sticking with the brand for replacement parts ensures that you maintain the integrity, warranty, and safety ratings of your original hardware. Avoid unbranded, third-party parts, as they may not meet the stringent safety and quality standards required for commercial door hardware, potentially leading to failure and liability.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
While most parts are small, consider the installation requirements. Components like electric power transfers (EPT) require precise mortising into the door and frame, so ensure you have the tools and space to install them correctly. For maintenance departments, stocking common, small replacement parts like dogging keys, end caps, and shafts is a smart strategy that doesn't require much storage space but saves significant downtime.

## What Is the Best Way to Choose von duprin doors?

Choosing the right Von Duprin parts involves a methodical approach to ensure you get the correct component for your specific needs. Follow these steps for the best results.

**Step 1: Identify Your Existing Hardware**
The first and most critical step is to identify the exact model and series of your Von Duprin device. Look for a label on the device, typically on the head case or push bar. Note the series number (e.g., 99, 22, 88) and any other identifying information. This will be your guide for finding compatible parts.

**Step 2: Diagnose the Problem Accurately**
Understand exactly what is broken or needs upgrading. Is an end cap cracked? Is the dogging mechanism stripped? Does the top latch fail to engage? A precise diagnosis prevents you from buying unnecessary parts. If you're unsure, consult a professional locksmith.

**Step 3: Consult Parts Manuals and Diagrams**
Use the model number to find the official Von Duprin parts manual or an exploded-view diagram online. These resources will show you every component and its corresponding part number. This is the most reliable way to identify the exact part you need, like the `050492` Top Latch Kit.

**Step 4: Prioritize OEM Parts**
Always choose genuine Von Duprin OEM parts. While aftermarket options may be cheaper, they often lack the quality control, precise fit, and material strength of the originals. For life-safety hardware, compromising on quality is not an option.

**Step 5: Consider the Environment**
Evaluate where the door is located. A back-of-house door has different needs than a main entrance that gets hit with carts all day. For high-abuse areas, invest in heavy-duty or impact-resistant versions of parts if available. For electrified hardware, ensure the specifications (voltage, function) match your access control system.
