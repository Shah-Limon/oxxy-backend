## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 6 Zone Sprinkler Controllers

Choosing the right sprinkler controller is crucial for an efficient irrigation system. Here are the key factors to consider to ensure you select the perfect model for your needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, assess your property. Do you have exactly 6 zones, or might you expand in the future? Some controllers are available in 4, 6, 8, or 12-zone models. Think about where you will install the unit. Do you need a weather-resistant outdoor model or can you place it indoors in a garage or basement? Also, consider your tech comfort level. Do you want the convenience of a Wi-Fi-enabled smart controller that you can manage from your phone, or do you prefer a simpler, non-connected dial or button interface?

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
6-zone sprinkler controllers have a wide price range. Basic, non-smart indoor models like the Orbit Easy Dial can be found for as little as $40-$60. Mid-range options, including durable outdoor models or simple smart controllers, typically fall between $70 and $120. High-end smart controllers with advanced features, premium apps, and brand names like Hunter can cost $150 or more, especially when you factor in optional accessories.
