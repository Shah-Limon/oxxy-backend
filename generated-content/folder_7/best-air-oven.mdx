## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Air Oven

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you start shopping, think about how you'll use your air oven. Are you a large family that needs to cook big meals, or a single person looking for quick, healthy options? Do you plan to bake, roast, air fry, or all of the above? Consider the types of dishes you cook most often. If you love rotisserie chicken, look for a model with that feature. If you're tight on counter space, a compact or multi-functional model like the Ninja Flip will be a better fit.
