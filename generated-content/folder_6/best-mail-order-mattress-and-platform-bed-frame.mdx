## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Mail Order Mattress And Platform Bed Frame

Choosing the right bed frame is just as important as choosing the right mattress. A good frame provides essential support, extends the life of your mattress, and contributes to the overall aesthetic of your bedroom. Here’s what you need to consider to make a smart purchase.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, think about how you use your bed. Do you just sleep in it, or do you also read, work, or watch TV? If you spend a lot of time sitting up, a frame with a comfortable, supportive headboard, like the [Allewie Faux Leather Platform Bed](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B09SHHJ49B?tag=home-essentials-guide-20&linkCode=osi&th=1&psc=1), is a great choice. Also, consider your mattress type. Memory foam and latex mattresses require a solid or closely slatted foundation to prevent sagging. Ensure the slat spacing on the frame is no more than 3 inches apart for optimal support.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Platform bed frames are available at a wide range of price points. You can find basic, functional metal frames like the [Best Price Mattress 14-Inch Platform](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07CVQXC66?tag=home-essentials-guide-20&linkCode=osi&th=1&psc=1) for around $70-$150.
