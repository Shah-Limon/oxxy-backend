## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Side By Side That Makes A Lot Of Ice

Choosing the right accessories to complement your ice-making setup is crucial. These products work 'side by side' with your main appliance to enhance your experience. Here is what to consider to ensure you get the most value and utility.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, identify your primary use for ice. Are you a meal prepper who needs to store large batches of soup? A silicone freezer tray is perfect. Do you spend weekends camping or tailgating? A high-performance cooler and leakproof ice packs are essential. Are you the life of the party? LED ice cubes will elevate your events. Understanding your lifestyle will guide you to the products that will serve you best. Don't buy a large cooler if you only need to pack a lunch for work.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
The products on our list range from affordable novelties to high-performance gear. You can get a pack of fun LED ice cubes or refreshing mints for under $20. High-quality freezer trays and portable coolers typically range from $25 to $60. Set a budget based on your needs and how frequently you will use the item. Investing a bit more in a durable, well-insulated cooler, for example, can save you money on ice and spoiled food in the long run.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Pay close attention to the features that matter for each product type. For coolers, look at insulation time (e.g., 18+ hours), capacity (e.g., 16 cans), and whether it is leak-proof. For freezer trays, consider the material (food-grade silicone), portion size markings, and if it comes with a lid. For reusable ice packs, check for leakproof seals and material durability. For novelty items like LED cubes, battery life is the most important spec.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Look for products made from high-quality, durable materials. For items that contact food, ensure they are BPA-free and made from food-grade plastic or silicone. For coolers and ice packs, look for reinforced seams, sturdy zippers, and robust outer materials like TPU that can withstand outdoor use. Reading user reviews can provide valuable insight into the long-term durability of a product. A well-made item will last for years, providing better value.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
While not all great products come from big names, a brand with a strong reputation for quality and customer service is often a safer bet. Brands like Ice Breakers are known for their consistent flavor, while newer, innovative brands like Ice Lock have built a following by solving a specific problem effectively. Research the brand and see if they stand behind their products with warranties or guarantees.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
Consider the physical size and portability of the item. A soft-sided cooler is much lighter and easier to store than a hard-sided one. Collapsible ice packs save a huge amount of space when not in use. Stackable freezer trays help keep your freezer organized. Think about where you will store these items in your home and how easy they will be to transport when you need them.
