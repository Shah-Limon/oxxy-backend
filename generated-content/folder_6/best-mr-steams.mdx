## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Mr Steams

Choosing the right steam shower generator and accessories is a significant investment in your home and well-being. To ensure you make the best choice, consider these crucial factors before making a purchase.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, accurately measure your shower enclosure. The volume of the space (Length x Width x Height in feet) is the primary factor in determining the required generator power (kW). Also, consider the materials. Natural stone like marble or granite requires a more powerful generator than non-porous materials like tile or acrylic. Will this be for daily relaxation, post-workout recovery, or occasional luxury? Your intended use will help you decide if you need features like fast-heating options or aromatherapy systems.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
A complete Mr. Steam system can range from around $1,500 for a basic, small generator-only setup to over $5,000 for a high-powered, feature-rich package. A standalone generator might cost between $1,200 and $3,000. Controls, steam heads, and accessories like AutoFlush or aromatherapy pumps can add another $500 to $2,000. Set a realistic budget that includes not only the components but also professional installation, which is highly recommended.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look for key features that enhance the experience and longevity of the system. A microprocessor control offers more precise temperature management than basic thermostats. An AutoFlush system is critical for automating maintenance and preventing mineral buildup. Consider the control type: do you want a simple digital control, or a more advanced one with programmable features and app connectivity? Also, check the generator's warm-up time; some models offer an "Express Steam" feature for near-instant readiness.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
The heart of the system is the generator, and its construction is paramount. Look for generators with stainless steel tanks and internal components. Stainless steel is highly resistant to corrosion and ensures a long service life. Check for certifications like UL (Underwriters Laboratories), which indicates the product has met rigorous safety standards. Robotically welded seams and serviceable heating elements are also signs of a high-quality, durable unit.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
Investing in a product from a reputable, established brand like Mr. Steam is crucial. They have a long history of engineering reliable products and offer robust warranties and customer support. An established brand will also have a reliable supply of replacement parts, ensuring your system can be serviced for years to come, protecting your investment. Avoid unknown or off-brand systems that may lack support and long-term reliability.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
While the system isn't portable, the generator's size and installation flexibility are key. Mr. Steam generators are known for their compact, briefcase-like size. This allows them to be installed in a variety of out-of-the-way locations, such as a closet, vanity, or insulated attic space, up to 60 feet from the shower. Measure your potential installation space to ensure the generator you choose will fit comfortably with adequate clearance for service access.

## What Is the Best Way to Choose mr steams?

Choosing the perfect Mr. Steam system involves a methodical approach to ensure it meets your specific needs and fits your space perfectly. Follow these steps for the best results.

**1. Calculate Your Shower Volume:**
Start by accurately measuring the length, width, and height of your shower enclosure in feet. Multiply these three numbers to get the total cubic footage (L x W x H = cu. ft.). This is the single most important factor in sizing your generator.

**2. Adjust for Materials and Features:**
Next, adjust the cubic footage based on your shower's construction. If your walls are natural stone (marble, granite, slate), multiply your cubic footage by 2.0. If they are ceramic or porcelain tile, multiply by 1.35. For each exterior wall or window, add an additional 10-20% to your calculation. This adjusted number will guide you to the correct generator kW rating. Mr. Steam provides an online calculator for this purpose.

**3. Select Your Generator:**
Using your final adjusted cubic footage, choose the appropriate generator model. It's always better to size up slightly if you are on the borderline between two sizes. A slightly more powerful generator will heat the space faster and work more efficiently than a smaller one that is constantly running at maximum capacity.

**4. Choose Your Controls and Finishes:**
Select a control package that matches your desired functionality and aesthetic. Options range from simple on/off and temperature controls (iTempo) to advanced touchscreen controls with app integration (iSteam). Ensure the finish (e.g., polished chrome, brushed nickel) matches your other bathroom fixtures.

**5. Consider Essential Accessories:**
Do not skip the accessories. The AutoFlush system is highly recommended for longevity, especially in areas with hard water. If you enjoy aromatherapy, add the AromaStream system. For music lovers, the AudioStream system can integrate speakers into your steam experience.

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
- **Under-sizing the generator:** This is the most common mistake and results in weak, unsatisfactory steam. Always use the adjusted cubic footage calculation.
- **Ignoring ventilation:** Proper ventilation outside the steam shower is crucial to manage moisture in the rest of the bathroom.
- **Improper sealing:** The steam shower must be a fully enclosed, waterproof space. Any gaps will allow steam to escape, reducing efficiency and potentially causing moisture damage elsewhere.

By following these steps and consulting with a professional installer, you can confidently choose a Mr. Steam system that will provide years of reliable, spa-quality relaxation.
