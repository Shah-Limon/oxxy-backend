## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Tequila For Jello Shots

Choosing the right supplies for your tequila jello shots can make a huge difference in preparation, presentation, and enjoyment. Here are the key factors to consider to ensure you get the best products for your needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, think about the type and size of your event. Are you hosting a small, casual get-together or a large, formal wedding? For a fun, themed party, neon or glitter cups might be perfect. For an elegant affair, simple, crystal-clear cups or those with a gold trim are more appropriate. Consider if you need lids for transportation or pre-making shots. If you make jello shots often, investing in a dispenser like the Jellinator could be a wise choice to save time and effort.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
Your budget will play a significant role. Basic, no-frills disposable cups in bulk can be very inexpensive, often ranging from $10-$20 for several hundred. Mid-range options, which might include more durable plastic, fun colors, or included lids, typically fall in the $20-$35 range. Premium products, like the innovative EZ-Squeeze cups or specialty dispensers, can cost more, but they offer features that greatly enhance the experience. Decide how much you are willing to spend based on your event's importance and your frequency of use.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look for features that add convenience and quality.
*   **Capacity:** The most common sizes are 1 oz and 2 oz. Choose based on how generous you want your servings to be.
*   **Lids:** Essential for stacking, storing, and transporting. Ensure they fit snugly to prevent leaks.
*   **Material:** Look for BPA-free, food-grade plastic. Sturdier plastics like polystyrene or heavy-duty polypropylene are less likely to crack.
*   **Special Features:** Consider unique designs like squeezable cups for easy consumption or backlight-reactive cups for glow parties.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Nobody wants a flimsy cup that cracks and spills a sticky mess. Look for terms like 'heavy-duty', 'crack-resistant', or 'hard plastic' in the product description. Reading user reviews can be very insightful here, as people will quickly point out if the cups are too thin. If you plan to reuse the cups, ensure they are sturdy enough to withstand hand washing.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
Brands like EZ-SQUEEZE and MATANA specialize in party supplies and often have a reputation for quality and innovative design. While a brand name is not everything, established manufacturers are often more reliable in terms of product safety, consistency, and customer service. They have more to lose from a poor-quality product, so they often maintain higher standards.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
If you are making jello shots for an event at a different location, portability is key. Cups with lids are a must. Stackability is another crucial factor, both for transport and for storage in your refrigerator and cupboards. A dispenser or a large box of 1000 cups will require dedicated storage space, so consider your available room before making a bulk purchase.

Choosing the best supplies for your tequila jello shots is a straightforward process if you follow a few key steps. This will ensure you get a product that matches your event, budget, and expectations.

**Step-by-Step Selection Guide:**

2.  **Determine Your Budget:** Decide on a price range. Are you looking for the most economical option possible, or are you willing to pay a little more for premium features like lids or a unique design?
3.  **Prioritize Key Features:** Make a list of must-have features. Is a 2 oz capacity important? Do you absolutely need lids for transport? Is a squeezable design a feature your guests would love? Focusing on your priorities will help you narrow down the options.
4.  **Read Reviews for Durability:** Once you have a few options, read user reviews with a focus on comments about quality and sturdiness. Look for recurring complaints about cracking or leaking, and favor products praised for being durable.
5.  **Consider the Full Kit:** Think beyond just the cups. Do you need a pre-made flavor mix to save time? Would a dispenser make your life easier for a very large batch? Sometimes buying a complete system or kit is the most efficient choice.
