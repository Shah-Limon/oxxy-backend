## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Air Fryer That Does Everything

Choosing the right multi-function air fryer involves more than just picking the one with the most features. Consider these key factors to ensure you invest in an appliance that perfectly suits your cooking style, kitchen space, and budget.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you buy, think about what you'll cook most often. Are you a large family that needs to cook whole chickens or big batches of fries? A model with a 6 to 10-quart capacity, like the Instant Pot or Cuisinart, would be ideal. If you're cooking for one or two, or have limited space, a compact 4 to 5-quart model like the Chefman or Ninja Pro is a better fit. Consider functions as well. If you love rotisserie chicken, the Instant Pot is your go-to. If you're all about maximum crispiness for frozen snacks, a Ninja with Max Crisp technology is the answer.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Air fryers that do everything can range in price significantly. You can find excellent budget-friendly models, like the Chefman, for under $100. Mid-range options from brands like Cosori and Ninja typically fall between $100 and $150, offering a great balance of features and performance. Premium models, especially those with large capacities or specialized functions like rotisserie, can cost $150 or more.
