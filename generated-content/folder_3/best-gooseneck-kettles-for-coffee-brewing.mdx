## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Gooseneck Kettles For Coffee Brewing

Choosing the right gooseneck kettle can dramatically improve your coffee brewing. To make the best choice, consider these key factors that distinguish a great kettle from an average one.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, determine how you'll use the kettle. Are you a dedicated pour-over coffee brewer, or do you also drink a lot of tea? If you do both, an electric kettle with variable temperature presets is invaluable.
