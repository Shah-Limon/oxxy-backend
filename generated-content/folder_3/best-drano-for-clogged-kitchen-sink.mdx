### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
The formula is the most critical feature.

*   **Foamers (e.g., Drano Dual-Force Foamer):** These expand to fill the entire pipe, making them excellent for cleaning pipe walls, removing odors, and for use in garbage disposals.
*   **Enzymes (e.g., Drano Max Build Up Remover):** These are for prevention, not for clearing active clogs. They use natural bacteria to slowly eat away at organic matter lining the pipes.
