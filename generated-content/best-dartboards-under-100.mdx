## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Dartboards Under 100

Choosing the right electronic dartboard involves more than just picking the one with the most games. To ensure you get the best value and a board that suits your needs, consider these important factors.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, think about who will be using the dartboard and where. Is it for serious practice, family game nights, or parties with friends? If kids will be playing, a soft-tip electronic board is the safest option. For social gatherings, look for boards that support a higher number of players (like 8). If you plan to move the board around or use it outdoors, a model with a battery power option is essential.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
While all the boards in this guide are under $100, there's still a range. Models in the $50-$70 range typically offer core features like multiple games and automatic scoring. As you approach the $100 mark, you'll find premium features like wooden cabinets, advanced lighting effects, enhanced displays, and more robust accessory packages. Set your budget based on the features that matter most to you.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look closely at the technical details.
*   **Game Variety:** How many games and variations are offered? While more is often better, ensure it includes the classics you love, like Cricket and 301/501.
