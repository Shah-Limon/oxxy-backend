## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Electric Knife Sharpeners For Wusthof

Choosing the right sharpener is crucial to protect your investment in Wusthof knives. Here are the key factors to consider to ensure you select a model that will deliver a perfect edge safely and effectively.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Wusthof knives are known for their Precision Edge Technology (PEtec), which creates a 14-degree angle per side (a 28-degree inclusive angle). The most important factor is choosing a sharpener that is compatible with this specific angle. Using a sharpener with a wider angle (like a traditional 20-degree sharpener) will dull your knife over time, while one with a much narrower angle could create a fragile edge. Look for models that specifically mention compatibility with 14 or 15-degree edges. Also, consider if you need to sharpen other types of knives, such as serrated, single-bevel, or sport knives.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
Knife sharpeners come in a wide price range. Simple manual pull-through models can be found for as little as $20-$40. Entry-level electric sharpeners, like the Presto EverSharp, typically fall in the $40-$70 range. High-performance electric models with multiple stages, diamond abrasives, and advanced features, such as the Wusthof or Chef'sChoice sharpeners, can range from $150 to over $200. Set a budget based on your needs, but remember that investing in a quality sharpener is an investment in the longevity of your knives.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
