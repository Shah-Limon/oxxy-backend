## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Air Fryer For 2 People

Choosing the right air fryer involves more than just picking the one that looks best. To ensure you're happy with your purchase, consider these key factors tailored for a two-person household.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you buy, think about what you'll cook most often. If you're mainly making fries and reheating leftovers, a simple, compact model will suffice. If you plan to cook full meals, like a protein and a vegetable simultaneously, consider a dual-basket model like the Ninja Foodi DZ201. For two people, a capacity between 3 to 5 quarts is generally the sweet spot. A 2-quart model is great for very small spaces and individuals, while anything over 6 quarts might be excessive unless you meal prep or entertain.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Air fryer prices can vary widely. You can find basic, effective models for as little as $50-$80. These are great for trying out air frying without a big investment.
