## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Ekena Millwork Display Shelves

Choosing the right decorative shelf involves more than just picking a design you like. To ensure you're happy with your purchase for years to come, consider these important factors.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, think about the shelf's purpose and location. Are you creating a focal point in the living room, adding storage in the bathroom, or displaying collectibles in a hallway? The function will dictate the size and style. A wide, classic shelf like the 36-inch model is great for a picture ledge, while a small, ornate shelf like the Hillsborough is perfect for a decorative accent. Also, match the shelf's style—be it classic, Victorian, or rustic farmhouse—to your existing home decor for a cohesive look.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Ekena Millwork products are known for their quality and design, and their prices reflect that. Expect to spend anywhere from $30 for a small, simple shelf to over $150 for larger, more ornate pieces or sets. While you can find cheaper shelves made of MDF or particleboard, investing in a solid urethane or reclaimed wood shelf means you are paying for superior durability, detail, and longevity. Set a budget that allows for a quality piece that you won't need to replace.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Pay attention to the material. Most of these shelves are solid urethane, which is lightweight, durable, and impervious to moisture—making it ideal for any room, including kitchens and bathrooms. Others are made from reclaimed wood, offering authentic rustic character. Also, consider the finish. Most urethane shelves come factory primed, meaning you will need to paint them. The reclaimed wood shelves come pre-finished. Finally, check the dimensions carefully, including width, height, and depth (projection), to ensure it fits your space and can hold your desired items.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Ekena Millwork's urethane products are molded from steel molds, which results in sharp, crisp details that mimic original wood carvings. This solid, high-density material is superior to coated MDF as it will not swell, rot, or crack. For the wood shelves, look for the term 'reclaimed wood', which indicates authentic, aged material with natural character, as opposed to new wood that has been artificially distressed.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
Ekena Millwork is a well-regarded brand in the architectural millwork industry. They are known for their historically accurate designs and use of high-quality, modern materials that offer advantages over traditional wood. Choosing a product from an established brand like Ekena ensures you are getting a well-made item that is backed by a reputation for quality and customer satisfaction.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
Before buying, measure your wall space carefully. Use painter's tape to outline the shelf's dimensions on the wall to get a visual sense of how it will fit. Consider both the width and the projection from the wall. A shelf that is too deep can be an obstruction in a narrow hallway, while one that is too small can look lost on a large wall. Also, think about the weight of the shelf and what you plan to display on it to ensure you use appropriate mounting hardware for your wall type.
