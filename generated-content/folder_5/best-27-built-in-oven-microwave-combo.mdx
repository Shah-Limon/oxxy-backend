## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 27 Built In Oven Microwave Combo

Choosing the right built-in oven microwave combo requires careful consideration of your kitchen space, cooking habits, and budget. This guide will walk you through the essential factors to ensure you select an appliance that fits perfectly and meets all your culinary needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, measure your cabinet cutout precisely. A "27-inch" model is designed to fit into a 27-inch wide cabinet, but the required height and depth can vary. Double-check the manufacturer's cutout specifications. Consider how you cook. If you frequently bake or roast, a model with true convection is essential. If you want to replace a standalone air fryer, look for a combo unit with a dedicated air fry mode and included accessories like a crisper basket.
