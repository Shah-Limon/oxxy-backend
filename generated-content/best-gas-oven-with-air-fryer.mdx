## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Gas Oven With Air Fryer

Choosing the right appliance requires careful consideration of your needs, space, and budget. Use this guide to help you navigate the key factors and make a choice you'll be happy with for years to come.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, determine what type of appliance best fits your kitchen and cooking style. Are you looking for a full-size, freestanding range like the FORNO Lazio that replaces your entire oven and cooktop? This is ideal for major kitchen upgrades and serious home cooks. Or, is a versatile countertop model like the Breville or Ninja a better fit? These are perfect for adding functionality without a major installation, and they are great for smaller households or as a secondary oven. Consider the types of meals you cook most often and the quantity of food you prepare.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
The price for ovens with air fryer functionality can vary dramatically. High-quality countertop models from brands like Cuisinart, Ninja, and Breville typically range from $200 to $500. These offer incredible versatility and performance for their size. For a full dual-fuel or gas range with an integrated air fryer, expect to invest significantly more, often from $1,500 to $3,000+. While the initial cost is higher, these units offer unparalleled power, capacity, and a fully integrated kitchen solution. If you're on a tight budget, consider an accessory like the OPENICE basket for under $30 to upgrade your existing oven.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look beyond the basic functions. For gas ranges, check the BTU output of the burners—higher BTUs mean faster heating. For all models, consider the convection system.
