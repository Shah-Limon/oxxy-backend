## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Footstools For Toilets

Choosing the right toilet stool can significantly impact your comfort and health. Before making a purchase, consider these key factors to ensure you select a product that perfectly meets your needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, assess your primary requirements. Measure the height of your toilet bowl from the floor to the rim; most standard toilets are 14-16 inches high, for which a 7-inch stool is generally recommended. Consider who will be using the stool. If it's for a family with varying heights, an adjustable model like the **Osivaex Foldable & Adjustable Stool** is ideal. If you have specific health concerns like hemorrhoids or severe constipation, the ergonomic benefits of any well-designed stool will be valuable.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Footstools for toilets come in a range of prices. Basic, fixed-height plastic models like the **Hoissuem Non-Slip Stool** are very affordable, typically falling in the $15-$25 range. Mid-range options, which may include features like foldability or cushioned tops, can cost between $25 and $40. Premium models, such as the elegant **TINAMO Acacia Wood Stool** or the ultra-comfortable **LUXE Comfort Stool**, can be $40 or more. Set a budget based on the features that are most important to you.
