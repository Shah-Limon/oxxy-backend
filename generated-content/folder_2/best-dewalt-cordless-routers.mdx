## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Dewalt Cordless Routers

Choosing the right cordless router is about matching the tool's capabilities to your specific needs. Consider these key factors to ensure you make a purchase you'll be happy with for years to come.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, assess the type of work you'll be doing. Are you a professional who needs a tool for all-day use on a job site, or a hobbyist working on weekend projects? For heavy-duty tasks, a model with a robust brushless motor and high-capacity battery is essential. For fine, detailed work like inlay or sign-making, prioritize a router that is lightweight, ergonomic, and has precise depth adjustment. Also, if you already own DeWalt 20V MAX* tools, sticking with a compatible router (whether from DeWalt or a third-party brand) will save you money on batteries and chargers.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($120-$500 ranges)
Dewalt cordless routers and compatible systems span a wide price range. A bare tool from a third-party brand might cost around $120, making it an excellent budget entry point if you have batteries. A standalone DeWalt router (tool only) typically falls in the $180-$250 range. For a complete kit with batteries and a charger, or a multi-tool combo set, expect to invest anywhere from $300 to $500.
