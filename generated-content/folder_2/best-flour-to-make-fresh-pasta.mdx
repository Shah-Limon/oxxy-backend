## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Flour To Make Fresh Pasta

Choosing the right flour is the most critical decision in pasta making. The type of flour you select will determine the texture, flavor, color, and workability of your dough. Here’s what to consider to ensure you pick the perfect bag for your culinary needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, decide what kind of pasta you want to make. Are you aiming for silky, delicate egg noodles and ravioli, or hearty, rustic shapes with a firm bite?
